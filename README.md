🚀 ClubCraft AI
Generative AI for Campus Creators

ClubCraft AI is a generative AI-powered campus content platform that helps students and clubs instantly create ready-made promotional materials for their events.

Enter event details and generate a complete promotion kit in seconds — no design or marketing skills required.

🎯 Problem

College students often struggle to promote events due to limited design skills, marketing knowledge, time, and budget. As a result, great ideas fail to reach the right audience.

ClubCraft AI solves this by providing an all-in-one AI platform that instantly generates professional-quality event promotion content.

⚙ How It Works

User enters event details (name, theme, audience, date).

Backend sends structured prompts to the AI model.

AI generates complete promotional content.

Bias checker ensures inclusive communication.

Content is displayed and saved to user history.

🚀 Impact

ClubCraft AI democratizes creativity by removing technical barriers. It reduces content creation time from hours to seconds, increases campus engagement, and enables every student to become a creator.
